About Me

This summer (2023) is my second semester in Rutgers University's online Master of Information program. Previously (2004–2022), I taught Computer Science at Stony Brook University. My concentration is (tentatively) Archives & Preservation (with a focus on the late 20th century explosion of software for personal computers), but that may change as a result of my interests in technology policy and advocacy.

My research interests are varied, and primarily concern:

  • Legal and ethical issues relating to technology (particularly privacy, misinformation, and intellectual property rights)
  • Computer Science education
  • Forensic software engineering (analysis and mitigation of software failures)
