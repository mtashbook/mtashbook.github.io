About Me

I am currently a part-time student in Rutgers University's online Master of Information program (with an expected graduation date of January 2026). Prior to this (2004–2022), I taught Computer Science at Stony Brook University. Officially, my concentration is Archives & Preservation (with Dr. Dalbello as my advisor), but I also have a strong interest in technology policy and advocacy (I support the Electronic Frontier Foundation and the Internet Archive with regard to privacy and copyright reform).

My research interests are varied, and primarily concern:

  • Legal and ethical issues relating to technology (particularly privacy, misinformation, and intellectual property rights)
  • The history of computing
  • Computer Science education
  • Forensic software engineering (analysis and mitigation of software failures)
